About me

Hi, my name is James and I work remotely as a data specialist at Collie Analytics, working primarily with small businesses and colleges to provide data based solutions. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Health Sciences from Concordia University Portland.

I’ve had a lifelong fascination with puzzles and pattern discovery. Before entering the data field formally, I was naturally implementing data collection and rough analysis in past professions. Now with a toolbelt of data related skills I've found joy in sorting, discovering, and resolving the puzzles found within a given dataset.

My obsession with puzzles does not contain itself to my working life but extends into all aspects of me. As a college track and field athlete, I found my puzzle in the precision required to throw a javelin or maintain velocity through a triple jump. In my social life, this has blossomed into running and playing within multiple dungeons and dragons games, always staying one step ahead of my players.
